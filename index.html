Our Mission Statement

We strive to create a collaborative, inclusive, and innovative environment where team members feel empowered to push boundaries and achieve excellence in their craft. Our mission is to deliver high-quality solutions that meet and exceed our clients' expectations.

Team Rules

  • Commit to meeting once a week, and be prepared to share at the meeting.
  • If we fall behind schedule we will set aside extra time when all or most members are free to catch up on the work.
  • We will meet weekly and decide the meeting time for the next week at the end of each week of work, to ensure that everyone is free for each meeting.
  • Be willing to share when something doesn't work as intended, only by helping each other can we succeed.
  • If someone falls behind we work together to achieve our goals, if they do not agree then we will reach out to the instructor.
  • Respect others' time, people may be busy and need extra time to properly implement, but do not let it become a pattern, as you then take away others' time.
  • When working together make our work our main priority that way we can spend time set aside on work properly focused.
